What is Collaborative Intelligence?

The practice of working with AI as a partner rather than commanding it as a tool — combining human strengths with AI strengths to achieve outcomes neither could accomplish alone.

Human Strengths
  • Strategic context & intent
  • Ethical judgment & accountability
  • Creativity & nuance
  • Quality control & decision-making
  • Domain expertise
AI Strengths
  • Speed of execution
  • Pattern recognition at scale
  • Consistency & breadth
  • Draft generation & formatting
  • Synthesis across large amounts of text

The RCTF Prompting Framework

R
Role
Who should the AI act as?
"You are a marketing consultant…"
C
Context
What background does it need?
"…for a sustainable fashion startup…"
T
Task
What specifically should it do?
"…announcing our new recycled fabric line…"
F
Format
What structure or style?
"…as a 200-word LinkedIn post."

Complete example: "You are a marketing consultant [R] for a sustainable fashion startup [C]. Write a 200-word LinkedIn post [F] announcing our new recycled fabric line [T]."

Understanding Hallucinations

Hallucination: When AI generates information that sounds plausible and confident but is incorrect or fabricated. AI predicts probable text — it doesn't retrieve verified facts.

High-Risk Examples
  • Invented statistics or research data
  • Fabricated quotes & citations
  • Incorrect product specifications
  • Non-existent companies or people
  • Wrong dates, figures, or legislation
Your Defence
  • Verify facts through independent sources
  • Check for logical consistency
  • Identify potential bias in framing
  • Use Perplexity for cited research
  • Never publish without fact-checking

Iteration: Prompting as Conversation

Iteration means refining AI outputs through successive interactions. Professional AI users typically iterate 2–4 times on important outputs. Treat prompting as a conversation, not a single command.

Typical Iteration Sequence

Initial
RCTF prompt
→
Assess
Evaluate output
→
Refine
"Make it more concise"
→
Final
Quality output

Poor prompting → generic, unusable results. Skilled prompting + iteration → high-quality drafts needing minimal editing.

Business Writing Challenge

Assignment 1 Preview

A realistic business writing task that mirrors Assignment 1. Apply everything from Hours 1 and 2 under conditions similar to assessed work.

  • Craft your initial prompt using the RCTF framework
  • Apply at least 2–3 iterations to refine the output
  • Critically evaluate for hallucinations and bias before finalising
  • Compare your output with a partner using a different AI tool
  • Document every prompt and iteration in your AI Prompt Log

Remember: You remain responsible for strategy, quality control, and decision-making. AI handles execution speed and initial drafts. This is augmentation, not automation.

AI Prompt Log: Document Your Process

Professional AI users treat documentation as a core practice. The AI Prompt Log captures your collaboration process for continuous improvement — and feeds directly into Assignment 1.

What to Record
  • The full prompt you used (RCTF)
  • Tool & model version
  • What worked and what didn't
  • Number of iterations needed
  • Edits made to the final output
